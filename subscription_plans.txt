Subscription Plans and Features
===============================

This document outlines the available subscription plans for the dashboard management system.

1. Free Plan
   - Price: $0.00
   - Description: Free plan with basic features
   - Features: Access to basic dashboard, limited products (max 5 products per Excel upload), total of 50 products to be added in total till plan expiry

2. Basic Plan
   - Price: $9.99
   - Description: Basic plan with more features
   - Features: Access to dashboard, unlimited products, basic analytics (max 15 products per Excel upload), total of 200 products to be added in total till plan expiry

3. Premium Plan
   - Price: $19.99
   - Description: Premium plan with all features
   - Features: All features, advanced analytics, priority support (max 50 products per Excel upload), total of 500 products to be added in total till plan expiry
